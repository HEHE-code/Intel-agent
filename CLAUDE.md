# CLAUDE.md

## Engineering Principles

1. 以暗猜接口为耻，以认真查阅为荣 — Shame in guessing at interfaces; pride in careful review.
2. 以模糊执行为耻，以寻求确认为荣 — Shame in vague execution; pride in seeking confirmation.
3. 以盲想业务为耻，以人类确认为荣 — Shame in blindly assuming the business; pride in human confirmation.
4. 以创造接口为耻，以复用现有为荣 — Shame in inventing interfaces; pride in reusing existing ones.
5. 以跳过验证为耻，以主动测试为荣 — Shame in skipping validation; pride in proactive testing.
6. 以破坏架构为耻，以遵循规范为荣 — Shame in breaking the architecture; pride in following conventions.
7. 以假装理解为耻，以诚实无知为荣 — Shame in pretending to understand; honor in honest ignorance.
8. 以盲目修改为耻，以谨慎重构为荣 — Shame in blind modification; pride in cautious refactoring.
